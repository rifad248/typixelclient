Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What is the typical turnaround time for a project?

Turnaround times vary depending on the scope and complexity of the project. Generally, logo designs take 3-5 business days, while more comprehensive branding projects can take 1-2 weeks. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

How many revisions are included in the package?

All our packages include at least 2-3 rounds of revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final design. Additional revisions can be accommodated for a small fee if needed.

Do you provide the source files for the designs?

Yes, all packages include the final design files in various formats suitable for both print and digital use. Premium packages also include the source files (AI, PSD, etc.) for future editing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, UPI, and digital wallets. Payment plans are available for larger projects.
